Engineering Projects In Christ

EPIC Robotz, Team 4415 is a Robotics team from Valley Christian High School located in Cerritos, CA.
Our team competes in the FIRST Robotics Competition in Los Angeles and Las Vegas.
Using advanced curriculum and deliberate hands-on project-based learning initiatives,
such as Robotics, CAD, 3D Printing, CNC Machine, Web Design, Public Speaking, and others,
we equip students with demonstrable skills that enable them to succeed in STEM careers.

FRC 2016: STRONGHOLD



Kick-Off Weekend

Our team officially kicked off the FRC 2016 season, "FIRST STRONGHOLD" with 40 EPIC students and 20 mentors.
We start to meet every school day (except Wednesday's) from 6:30 to 9pm, as well as every Saturday at VCHS Building B.


Highlight of FRC 2015 Season


Las Vegas Regional 3/25/15 - 3/28/15

We ended the competition season strong at the Las Vegas Regional last weekend. Out of 44 teams at the competition, we were chosen by one of the top teams as a partner, ultimately advancing to the semifinals and finishing strong in 3rd place. (The team that picked us was from Phoenix and recently had a movie made about them titled “Spare Parts,” with George Lopez playing the role of the lead mentor who helped his team of underprivileged youth take on MIT in an underwater robotics competition and win.) Our unique robot design proved to be a valuable complement to the partnership, and they were proud of their ability to contribute.



Los Angeles Regional 3/11/15 - 3/14/15

EPIC Robotz made it into the Finals (top 8 alliances of 66 teams) in our second season, facing teams with over 15 years of FRC experience. We ended the Regional tied for 4th, missing the semi-finals by just one point! See a video of our best match. Students gained hands-on knowledge throughout the season as they worked with more than a dozen wonderful mentors. Congratulations to Head Coach Mr. Paul DeVries, and to all of our 44 "epic" students! We covets your prayers as we prepare to head to the Las Vegas Regional on March 25.
