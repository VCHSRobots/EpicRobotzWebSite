FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) was founded by Dean Kamen, an inventor and entrepreneur. His goal when the program started in 1989, was to inspire young people to be excited about science and technology. Since then, the non-profit charity has grown and developed innovative programs that motivate young people to pursue an education in the fields of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), while building self-confidence.

Mission

“Our mission is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.” (FIRST Website)

Gracious Professionalism

One thing FIRST highly values is the idea of “Gracious Professionalism.” Gracious Professionalism is an idea that promotes learning and quality work with respect towards one’s peers and competitors.

Coopertition®

“Coopertition® produces innovation. ” (FIRST Website)

Another value of FIRST is the notion of Coopertition. Coopertition is a display of ultimate sportsmanship. Though one might be my competitor, I should still show them respect. This notion is founded on the principle that teams should help and cooperate with one another, even thought they are in competition with one another.
All information is a summary of a more complete description that can be found on FIRST’s website (www.usfirst.org).
