At Valley Christian Schools, students have the opportunity to pursue science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) in their everyday course work, as well as extra curricular programs like FRC Robotics, CAD and 3D Printing. Engaging in unique STEM educational programs provides invaluable experience for students, cultivating them as future STEM professionals.
One of the many benefits of STEM education is the integration and application of cross-curricular content. This allows students to understand the relationship of the subjects they study and helps students answer the questions "Why do I need to know STEM education?” and "Where will I ever use STEM education?”
Growing tomorrow’s technology leaders today!
